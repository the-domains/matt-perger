Diameter

A tamper’s diameter is very important, but it’s only
one part of the story. Along
with diameter, one

should be measuring the effective tamping
area.

That is, the
size of the area that’s
actually

compressing the coffee grounds. At first this
seems
quite simple, but almost all tamper

manufacturers

include a curved edge
or ‘fillet’ on the bottom of
their tampers. This feature
reduces
their effective

tamping area by a startling
amount. These

diagrams will help you to
understand
how a tiny
curve can have a big impact on your tamper’s

effectiveness. Keep in mind
that all
of
them are to

scale, and assume that all
VST baskets have an
internal diameter of 58.7mm.
